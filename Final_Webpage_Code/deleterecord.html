AU Student Database

In order to add a record, just click the "Add Record" button and a record will be added.
In order to delete a record, just click the "Delete Record" button and a record will be deleted.
In order to update a record, just click the "Update Record" button and a recored will be updated.
If you wish to query the database, just click the query button.
To view what the tables look like, hover over the "Table" tab and select which table you wish to view.
